## Article 1 (Purpose)
These Terms govern the rights, obligations, and responsibilities between Chronos Health (the "Company") and members ("Members") regarding the use of the AI-based health prediction and digital health services provided by the Company (the "Service").

## Article 2 (Definition of the Service and Medical Disclaimer)
1. The Service provides **prediction reports**, **risk estimates**, **vitality scores**, **Five Ages**, and similar features based on health data input by the Member (survey responses, physical measurements, family history, routine logs, etc.).
2. **All screens and outputs of the Service are not medical practice and are for health-improvement reference only.** None of the prediction results provided by the Company can replace examination, diagnosis, or treatment prescription, and any medical decision must be made only after consultation with a medical professional.
3. The Service's prediction results are statistical estimates and are always presented together with a confidence interval. They do not represent a definitive medical judgment for any individual case.
4. By signing up for the Service, the Member is deemed to have sufficiently acknowledged and consented to the medical disclaimer above. Even where the disclaimer is not repeatedly shown in the Service after sign-up, the Member acknowledges that the disclaimer in this Article applies equally to every screen, report, and feature.

## Article 3 (Member Eligibility)
1. Only natural persons **aged 19 or older** may register as Members.
2. Members must provide accurate information at sign-up; any disadvantage arising from false information is borne by the Member.
3. The Company may refuse or terminate membership in the following cases:
   - When the Member is found to be under 19
   - When the Member registered by improper means (macros, automation tools, etc.)
   - When the Member has not consented to these Terms

## Article 4 (Account and Password)
1. Members use their email address as the Member identifier, and one account per person is the rule.
2. The password is set and managed by the Member, and the Company stores it as a PBKDF2-SHA256 hash (plain-text storage is prohibited).
3. Members are obliged not to share their password with third parties, and damages from password disclosure are borne by the Member.

## Article 5 (Use of the Service)
1. Members may use the Service for the following purposes:
   - Reviewing their own health prediction reports
   - Monitoring their vitality score and Five Ages
   - Recording routines (meals, exercise, sleep)
   - Community activity (posts, comments, likes)
   - Earning points and discounts on partner products
2. Members shall not engage in the following acts:
   - Posts containing expressions of medical practice (diagnosis, prescription, treatment)
   - Posting the personal or medical information of others
   - Direct sales or advertising of pharmaceuticals or supplements
   - Unauthorized access to Company systems, reverse engineering, or use of automation tools
3. Upon violation of the above, the Company may remove posts, temporarily suspend, or permanently terminate the account.

## Article 6 (CHRO Token and Reward System)
1. The Company accrues **pilot operation points** for Member activities (survey completion, 7-day routine streak, community posting, etc.).
2. **Points are not redeemable for cash** and may only be used for discounts on partner products and services designated by the Company.
3. The Company is reviewing a plan to convert pilot points to $CHRO tokens 1:1 at a future point (P5 mainnet launch); **this is not a promise, and the timing and conditions of implementation will be announced separately**.
4. Member registration or accrual of points does not guarantee future receipt of tokens.

## Article 7 (Community Operation)
1. The Company operates a keyword filter and report-based review for community posts and comments.
2. Violations of the medical disclaimer, advertising, promotion, profanity, discrimination, and hate speech may be blocked in advance or removed after the fact.
3. External video URLs are permitted only for hosts designated by the Company (YouTube, Vimeo, etc.).

## Article 8 (Personal Information Protection)
1. The Company processes the Member's personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act and the Service's Privacy Policy.
2. The Company stores personal information separately as **identification data (name, email, phone, nationality, etc.)** and **analysis data (survey responses, prediction results, routines, community activity)**.
3. Analysis data is processed only with a pseudonymized identifier (pseudonym ID) and does not directly identify the individual Member.

1. The Company may change the contents of the Service or suspend it temporarily for operational or technical reasons.
2. Material changes are announced 7 days before they take effect; if the Member does not consent, the Member may withdraw from membership.

2. The Company's liability for damages is limited to the amount paid by the Member to the Company (if any).
